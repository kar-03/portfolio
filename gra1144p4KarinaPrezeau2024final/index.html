Your Support Makes a Difference

Donations from generous people like you help the Arsht Center connect people of all ages to the arts and to each other through shared experiences in our theaters and through participation in our inclusive, nationally recognized arts education and community programs. Through the power of collective giving, every gift – no matter the amount – helps transforms lives. The Arsht Center is a mission-based 501(c)3 non-profit organization, and ticket sales only cover a portion of our costs. Support the Center’s vital, life-changing work with a gift today.
